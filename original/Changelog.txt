Blocks

- Added Fallen Leaf Block Variations
- Added Cactus Flower Color Variations
- Added Dry Grass Variations
- New Dragon Egg Texture
- New Quartz Block Color Palette
- Dark Oak Leaves Are Darker and Share a Texture with Pale Oak

- New Sound for the Pale Tree Trunk

Entities
====================

- The textures of the Ghast and Happy Ghast have been adjusted to be accurate to their size.
- A small Easter egg has been added with the Ghastling being renamed "Baby".
- The Wither now has a small outline.
- The Wither has new particles when entering its second phase.
- New texture and details with the charged Creeper.
- The Blaze has a new texture and outline on its model.
- The Squid and Turtle have new models for their baby variations.
- New model for the Creaking.
- New model for the Cows.
- Baby versions of the new variants added (Spring to Life).
- Added Technoblade nametag and custom saddle.
* Temporarily removed the extra MCEarth variants (sheep, pigs, cows) to avoid conflicting with the new variants in the Spring to Life update.
